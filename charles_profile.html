Exploratory Research

Constructing a Big Corpus of Data Using LLM to Feed Generative AI

Sreekumar NR, Narendran SM, Charles Jeyaseelan, Rahul Ganesh S

IEEE CCEM Pre-Conference Workshop 2024 Presentation

Proposed a methodology to train a large language model (LLM) to generate a high-quality corpus of religious texts, maintaining reliability and sanctity. The corpus is designed to improve generative AI's ability to produce contextually accurate and culturally sensitive text.

Contextual Semantic Analysis

Sreekumar NR, Narendran SM, Lasya S, Charles Jeyaseelan

IEEE CCEM Pre-Conference Workshop 2024 Presentation

Introduced a novel approach to calculate semantic similarity scores for unstructured text across 8 contextual domains. Leveraged semantic tree transformations and deep learning regressors to predict contextual scores, aiming to bridge gaps in lexicon-based methods.

Emotional Quotient: An AI-Based Approach Using Prolog

Sreekumar NR, Narendran SM, Charles Jeyaseelan

IEEE CCEM Pre-Conference Workshop 2024 Presentation

Proposed a method to estimate Emotional Quotient (EQ) using Prolog programming, focusing on six basic emotions (happy, sad, anger, fear, disgust, surprise). Designed a scoring mechanism for EQ estimation and multi-variate emotion annotation for predictive analysis.
